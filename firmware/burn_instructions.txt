Instructions to burn firmware on ArduBooster v1.0 board:

- Using an Arduino Uno (or compatible) board as a burner, to upload firmware to ArduBooster (Arduino as USB-to-Serial).
- Remove uC from Arduino board.
- Wire ArduBooster reset pin to Arduino reset pin.
- Wire ArduBooster Tx to Arduino Rx.
- Wire ArduBooster Rx to Arduino Tx.
- Connect Arduino Gnd and 5V to ArduBooster.
- On Arduino IDE, select the board according to used on ArduBooster (Arduino Uno recommended).
- BURN!!!
